Security & Privacy

HIPAA Compliance

At Doktura.ai, safeguarding your Protected Health Information (PHI) is our highest priority. Learn how we keep your data secure.

Our Commitment to Privacy

Doktura.ai is fully committed to maintaining the confidentiality, integrity, and availability of all Protected Health Information (PHI) we handle. We have built our AI infrastructure and tele-health platform from the ground up with compliance and security in mind, strictly adhering to the Health Insurance Portability and Accountability Act (HIPAA) of 1996 and its subsequent amendments, including the HITECH Act.

How We Ensure Compliance

1. Data Encryption

  • In Transit: All data transmitted between your device and our servers, including chat interactions with our AI and video calls with doctors, is encrypted using industry-standard TLS 1.2 or higher protocols.
  • At Rest: All stored PHI is encrypted using AES-256 encryption. Our databases are secured within private networks that are not directly accessible from the public internet.

2. Strict Access Controls

  • Role-Based Access: Only authorized personnel with a legitimate business need can access systems containing PHI.
  • Authentication: We enforce strong, multi-factor authentication (MFA) for all administrative and medical staff accessing the Doktura.ai backend systems.
  • Audit Logs: Comprehensive audit logs are maintained to track all access to, and modifications of, PHI. These logs are regularly reviewed to detect and prevent unauthorized activity.

3. Safe AI Processing

When you interact with our AI Assistant, your information is processed securely. Our AI models do not use your personal identifiable health data to train public models. Information is analyzed in real-time within our secure environment solely to provide you with immediate, accurate health guidance.

4. Third-Party Audits & Business Associate Agreements

We routinely undergo rigorous third-party security audits and penetration testing to identify and mitigate potential vulnerabilities. Furthermore, we execute strict Business Associate Agreements (BAAs) with all of our third-party service providers and cloud hosting partners to ensure they also meet stringent HIPAA requirements.

Your Rights Under HIPAA

Under HIPAA, you have the right to:

  • Request access to your medical records and PHI.
  • Request corrections to any inaccuracies in your health information.
  • Receive an accounting of disclosures of your PHI.
  • Request restrictions on how your PHI is used and disclosed.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact our privacy team using the information provided below.
