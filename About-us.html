About Us

About Doktura.Ai

At Doktura.Ai, we believe healthcare guidance should be fast, simple, private, and accessible to everyone.

We are an AI-powered healthcare platform designed to help people better understand their symptoms, ask health-related questions, and explore the right next steps anytime, anywhere. Whether you are feeling unwell late at night, unsure if you need to see a doctor, or simply looking for quick health guidance, Doktura.Ai is here to support you.

Our AI assistant provides instant, easy-to-understand health insights for everyday concerns. When professional medical care is needed, Doktura.Ai can help connect users with licensed doctors in Malaysia for online consultations, prescriptions, referrals, and follow-up support.

Our Mission

Our mission is to make healthcare more accessible by combining the speed of AI with the care of real doctors.

We aim to reduce waiting time, improve access to trusted health guidance, and help users make informed decisions about their health — without complicated steps, long queues, or unnecessary stress.

What We Do

Doktura.Ai helps users:

  • Understand symptoms and possible causes
  • Get instant health guidance and suggested next steps
  • Know when to use AI and when to consult a doctor
  • Connect with qualified doctors for medical care
  • Book online or clinic-based consultations
  • Keep health conversations private and secure

Our AI is designed for quick guidance and general health support. It does not replace a licensed doctor, especially for diagnosis, prescriptions, emergencies, or ongoing medical conditions.

Why Doktura.Ai?

Healthcare should not feel confusing or out of reach. Doktura.Ai makes it easier to start the healthcare journey by giving users a safe space to ask questions, understand their condition, and take action when needed.

With 24/7 access, secure digital records, and doctor consultation support, we help bridge the gap between online health information and real medical care.

Privacy and Trust

Your privacy matters to us. Doktura.Ai is built with confidentiality and data protection in mind, with conversations handled securely and in line with PDPA standards.

We are committed to creating a safe, reliable, and user-friendly healthcare experience for individuals and families across Malaysia.

Our Vision

We envision a future where quality healthcare support is available to everyone, regardless of time, location, or background.

By combining AI technology with real doctor access, Doktura.Ai is working toward a smarter, faster, and more convenient healthcare experience.
