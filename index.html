Can the AI replace a real doctor?

No, our AI is designed to provide quick guidance and health insights, but it cannot replace a licensed professional for accurate medical diagnosis and treatment. Always consult a real doctor for serious concerns.

Is my personal health information safe?

Yes, your privacy is our top priority. All conversations are completely confidential, encrypted, and HIPAA compliant. We do not sell your personal data.

When should I see a real doctor?

You should see a real doctor if you experience severe symptoms, need a medical prescription, require an official diagnosis, or have an ongoing condition that needs professional management.

How much does it cost?

Chatting with our AI assistant is completely free! If you decide you need professional care, consultations with a real qualified doctor start from just RM 38.00.

Can I book both online and in-clinic consultations?

Absolutely. Our platform gives you the flexibility to easily book secure video consultations or schedule in-person visits to verified nearby clinics, whichever works best for you.
