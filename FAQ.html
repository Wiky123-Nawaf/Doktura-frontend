Frequently Asked Questions

1. What is Doktura.Ai?

Doktura.Ai is an AI-powered healthcare support platform that helps users ask health-related questions, understand symptoms, and get general health guidance. When needed, users may also connect with licensed doctors for professional medical care.

2. Is Doktura.Ai a doctor?

No. Doktura.Ai AI assistant is not a doctor and does not replace professional medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment. It provides general health guidance only. For proper medical care, you should consult a qualified doctor.

3. Can Doktura.Ai diagnose my illness?

No. Doktura.Ai can help you understand possible causes of your symptoms and suggest next steps, but only a licensed doctor can provide a medical diagnosis.

4. When should I consult a doctor?

You should consult a doctor if your symptoms are serious, getting worse, lasting for a long time, or if you are unsure about your condition. For urgent or emergency situations, please contact emergency services or visit the nearest hospital immediately.

5. Can I talk to a real doctor through Doktura.Ai?

Yes. Doktura.Ai may allow users to connect with licensed doctors for online consultations, depending on availability and service location.

6. Can I get medicine or a prescription?

Prescriptions can only be provided by a licensed doctor after a proper consultation. The AI assistant cannot prescribe medicine.

7. Is my information private?

Yes. Doktura.Ai is designed with privacy and data protection in mind. Your health conversations and personal information are handled securely according to applicable privacy standards.

8. Do I need to create an account?

Some features may be available without an account, while others may require registration, especially if you want to book a doctor consultation or save consultation records.

9. Is Doktura.Ai free to use?

Some AI guidance features may be free. Doctor consultations or additional healthcare services may require payment. Any fees will be shown before you confirm the service.

10. Can children use Doktura.Ai?

Users under 18 should use Doktura.Ai with the involvement and consent of a parent or legal guardian.

11. Can I use Doktura.Ai in an emergency?

No. Doktura.Ai is not an emergency service. If you are facing a medical emergency, please call emergency services or go to the nearest hospital immediately.

12. How accurate is the AI guidance?

Doktura.Ai aims to provide helpful and easy-to-understand health guidance, but AI responses may not always be complete or suitable for your specific condition. Always consult a qualified doctor for serious or uncertain health concerns.

13. What kind of questions can I ask?

You can ask general health questions, describe symptoms, ask about possible next steps, or learn when you should speak to a doctor.

14. Can Doktura.Ai help me book a consultation?

Yes, where available, Doktura.Ai can help you connect with doctors for online or clinic-based consultations.

15. How do I get started?

Simply start a chat with Doktura.Ai, describe your concern, and follow the guidance provided. If needed, you can choose to connect with a qualified doctor for further care.
